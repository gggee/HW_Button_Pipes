The largest airplane

The An-225 is the heaviest cargo aircraft ever to take to the air. The only one an aircraft that surpasses the An-225 in terms of wingspan is the Hughes H-4 Hercules, which belongs to the class flying boats and took to the air only once in 1947.

The largest ship

Pioneering Spirit is the largest ship in the world involved in the transportation of offshore drilling platforms and laying pipelines under water. The ship is owned by a Swiss company, but sails the sea open spaces under the flags of Malta and Panama.
